📋 Terms & Conditions

AgriShare Platform — Terms & Conditions

1. Eligibility & Registration
You must be at least 18 years of age and a verified Indian resident to transact on AgriShare. All users must complete Aadhaar-based KYC before initiating any payment.

2. Token Ownership
Each token represents 1/5th fractional ownership of the listed equipment. Token holders share usage rights, maintenance costs, and insurance premiums equally. Tokens are non-transferable without AgriShare approval.

3. Equipment Rental
The renter is responsible for the equipment during the rental period. Any damage beyond normal wear will be deducted from the security deposit. Equipment must be returned in the same condition as received.

4. Labour Hire
AgriShare acts solely as a marketplace. Workers are independent contractors. The platform fee of 8% covers booking management and dispute resolution only. Farmers must ensure a safe working environment.

5. Payments & Refunds
All transactions are processed securely via RBI-compliant gateways. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days. Security deposits are refunded within 48 hours of verified equipment return.

6. Cancellation Policy
Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the booking start date are fully refunded. Cancellations within 48 hours attract a 25% fee. No-shows forfeit the full booking amount.

7. Dispute Resolution
All disputes shall be resolved through AgriShare's internal arbitration mechanism. Unresolved disputes will be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka.

8. Privacy
Your personal data is processed in accordance with AgriShare's Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws. We do not sell your information to third parties.
